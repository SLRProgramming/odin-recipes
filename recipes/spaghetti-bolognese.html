Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic pasta dish with a rich
tomato and meat sauce. Simply brown minced beef with 
onions and garlic, add tomatoes and herbs, then simmer 
before serving over spaghetti with Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chopped tomatoes
    • Tomato purée Dried herbs (oregano or basil) OLive oil Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. Fry onion and garlic, add minced beef, brown well.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato purée, herbs; simmer.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti with Parmesan.
