Chicken curry

Chicken curry is a warming dish with tender chicken pieces in a spiced,
creamy sauce. Brown chicken, add onions and garlic, stir in curry powder 
and tomatoes, then simmer with coconut milk before serving with rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (breast or thigh)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Curry powder
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Oil
  • Fresh coriander (optional)

Steps

  1. Fry onion and garlic, add chicken, brown well.
  2. Stir in curry powder, tomatoes, coconut milk; simmer.
  3. Serve with rice and garnish with coriander.
