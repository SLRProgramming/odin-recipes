Vegetable stir-fry

A quick and healthy stir-fry with mixed vegetables tossed in soy sauce 
and garlic. Simply fry your choice of veg, add soy sauce, 
and serve with noodles or rice.

Ingredients

  • MIxed vegetables (e.g., peppers, carrots, broccoli)
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Noodles or rice

Steps

  1. Heat oil, fry garlic and vegetables quickly.
  2. Add soy sauce, toss until coated.
  3. Serve with rice or noodles.
